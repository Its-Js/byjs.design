# Books

Please go through the reviews on goodreads and the reddit books list before making any purchases.

Heres a list of books on design that I read/own with their goodreads link:
- [The Design of Everyday Things](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/840.The_Design_of_Everyday_Things) by Don Norman
- [Grid Systems](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/350962.Rastersysteme_f_r_die_visuelle_Gestaltung_Grid_systems_in_Graphic_Design?ref=nav_sb_ss_1_12) by Josef Muller-Brockman
- [The Designer's Dictionary of Color](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/31305530) by Sean Adams
- [The Designer's Dictionary of Type](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/42293286-the-designer-s-dictionary-of-type?ref=nav_sb_ss_1_33) by Sean Adams
- [Information Architecture](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/70132.Information_Architecture_for_the_World_Wide_Web?ref=nav_sb_ss_1_24) by Louis Rosenfeld, Peter Morville & Jorge Arango
- [Web Style Guide](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/29101737-web-style-guide?ref=nav_sb_ss_1_15) by Patrick J. Lynch & Sarah Horton
- [This is Learning Experience Design](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/60877690-this-is-learning-experience-design) by Niels Floor

Also, check out the [recommended readings]((https://www.reddit.com/r/UXDesign/wiki/books/)) by the [r/uxdesign](https://www.reddit.com/r/UXDesign/) community on reddit!
